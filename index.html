This project is built upon the research paper and repository SinGAN, developed by Tamar Rott Shaham, Tali Dekel, and Tomer Michaeli, available at paper , repo.
This project is the result of Harel Mendelman's personal endeavor, undertaken exclusively for the purpose of study.
In this project, my primary aim was to enhance the original SinGAN architecture, with a particular focus on elevating the super-resolution aspect of SinGAN.
This website was created to ensure a comprehensive and organized presentation of my research results.
